EduPay is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our school fees payment platform.

We collect data necessary to process payments and maintain service integrity.

2.1 Identity and Contact Data

  • Parent/Sponsor/Student Name: For account identification.
  • Phone Number & Email: Essential for OTP verification, digital receipts, and M-Pesa transactions.

2.2 Financial and Transaction Data

  • Amount Paid, Date, Transaction ID: Stored in our MongoDB database for audit trails.
  • School/Admission Number: Used to link the payment to the correct student account.

2.3 Technical Data

Collected via Node.js/Express logs: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage timestamps for security and performance analysis.

We process your data based on the necessity for contract performance (fee payment) and legitimate interest (security).

  • To process and verify school fee payments via M-Pesa API.
  • To generate and send digital receipts (Email/WhatsApp).
  • To maintain account security using JWT and bcrypt authentication.

We only share data with necessary parties:

  • Educational Institutions: Transaction details are shared with your designated school for reconciliation purposes.
  • Payment Gateways: Necessary financial data is shared with M-Pesa and bank processors to execute the payment.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) in Kenya.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

We use strong security measures across our stack:

  • Database: Data stored in MongoDB Atlas is secured with access controls and encryption.
  • In Transit: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Authentication: User credentials are never stored in plain text; we use bcrypt hashing.

Under the Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to access, rectify, or object to the processing of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
