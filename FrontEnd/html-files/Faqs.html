Payment and Transaction Queries

EduPay supports M-Pesa (PayBill/Lipa na M-Pesa), Bank Transfers, and **Card Payments. We aim to give you flexibility for your convenience.

Payments processed via M-Pesa or Card are typically real-time or reflect within minutes. You will receive an instant digital receipt as confirmation.

If the amount was deducted but no receipt was issued, **do not attempt a second payment. Contact our support team immediately via WhatsApp or our Contact Form with your M-Pesa transaction code for verification.

Once logged into your Parent/Sponsor Dashboard, navigate to the "Fees Management" section to view all past transactions and download digital receipts.
